Skill nay lam duoc gi?

Khi ban yeu cau Antigravity tao mot trang web hoac thiet ke giao dien, Skill UI/UX Pro Max se tu dong:

1. Chon phong cach thiet ke
Dua tren loai san pham (SaaS, e-commerce, y te...), skill se goi y phong cach phu hop nhu Glassmorphism, Minimalism, Dark Mode, v.v.
2. De xuat bo mau
Tu dong chon mau Primary, Secondary, CTA, Background, Text sao cho phu hop voi nganh va muc dich su dung.
3. Chon font chu
Goi y cap font Heading + Body tu Google Fonts, bao gom link import va config cho Tailwind CSS.
4. Kiem tra chuan UX
Ap dung 35+ quy tac UX tu dong: accessibility, responsive, touch target, animation, contrast ratio...
Meo: Ban khong can nho tat ca quy tac. Chi can noi voi Antigravity "tao trang web cho spa" hoac "thiet ke dashboard cho SaaS" - Skill se tu dong ap dung moi thu.

Huong dan su dung

Co 2 cach chinh de su dung Skill nay:

Cach 1: Tu dong (Khuyen dung)

Khi ban yeu cau Antigravity tao web, Skill se tu dong chay --design-system de tao toan bo he thong thiet ke. Ban chi can mo ta san pham:

"Tao trang landing page cho ung dung quan ly tai chinh ca nhan"
"Thiet ke giao dien dashboard cho phan mem quan ly nha hang"
"Tao website portfolio cho nhien anh gia"

Antigravity se tu dong: phan tich yeu cau, chon style + mau + font + UX rules, roi ap dung vao code.

Cach 2: Tim kiem thu cong (Nang cao)

Neu ban muon kiem soat chi tiet hon, dung lenh search.py voi cac tuy chon:

# Tao design system day du
python search.py "beauty spa wellness" --design-system -p "My Project"

# Tim phong cach cu the
python search.py "glassmorphism dark" --domain style

# Tim bo mau theo nganh
python search.py "healthcare medical" --domain color

# Tim font phu hop
python search.py "elegant luxury" --domain typography

# Tim quy tac UX
python search.py "animation accessibility" --domain ux

8 Nhom Quy Tac Thiet Ke

Skill su dung 35+ quy tac duoc chia thanh 8 nhom, sap xep theo muc do quan trong. Khi thiet ke, cac quy tac CRITICAL se duoc uu tien ap dung truoc.

Luu y: Cac quy tac nay khong chi la ly thuyet. Khi ban yeu cau tao web, Antigravity se tu dong kiem tra va ap dung chung vao code that.

1. Accessibility - Kha nang tiep can (CRITICAL)

Day la nhom quy tac quan trong nhat, dam bao moi nguoi deu co the su dung giao dien cua ban - ke ca nguoi khuyet tat.

Quy tacY nghia & Cach lam
color-contrastMau chu va mau nen phai co do tuong phan toi thieu 4.5:1. Vi du: chu trang tren nen den la OK, chu xam nhat tren nen trang la KHONG OK.
focus-statesKhi nguoi dung dung phim Tab de di chuyen, phai thay ro phan tu nao dang duoc chon (vien sang xung quanh).
alt-textMoi hinh anh co y nghia phai co mo ta (thuoc tinh alt). Trinh doc man hinh se doc mo ta nay cho nguoi khuyet tat thi giac.
form-labelsMoi o nhap lieu (input) phai co nhan (label) kem theo. Viet <label for="email">Email</label> thay vi chi de placeholder.
keyboard-navThu nghiem: ban co the dung trang web chi bang phim Tab va Enter khong? Neu khong duoc, can sua lai.

2. Touch & Tuong tac (CRITICAL)

Nguoi dung mobile cham bang ngon tay, khong dung chuot. Cac quy tac nay dam bao tuong tac muot ma tren moi thiet bi.

Quy tacY nghia & Cach lam
touch-targetMoi nut bam, link phai co kich thuoc toi thieu 44x44px. Ngon tay lon hon con tro chuot rat nhieu.
cursor-pointerMoi phan tu co the click phai co cursor: pointer de nguoi dung biet no bam duoc.
hover-feedbackKhi re chuot vao nut/card, phai co phan hoi truc quan: doi mau, bong do, hoac hieu ung nhe.
loading-buttonsKhi dang xu ly (gui form, tai du lieu), phai vo hieu hoa nut va hien trang thai loading.
error-feedbackThong bao loi phai xuat hien gan cho bi loi, khong phai nho len hang dau trang.

3-4. Hieu nang & Layout (HIGH)

Trang web nhanh va hien thi tot tren moi man hinh.

Quy tacHuong dan
image-optimizationDung dinh dang WebP, them loading="lazy" cho anh duoi fold. Dung srcset cho responsive images.
reduced-motionKiem tra prefers-reduced-motion - tat animation cho nguoi nhay cam voi chuyen dong.
viewport-metaLuon co <meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1">
font-sizeChu tren mobile toi thieu 16px. Nho hon se kho doc va Safari se tu dong zoom vao.
no-horizontal-scrollKHONG BAO GIO de trang web cuon ngang tren mobile. Kiem tra bang overflow-x: hidden.

5-6. Typography, Mau sac & Animation (MEDIUM)

Quy tacHuong dan
line-heightBody text dung line-height 1.5-1.75. Qua sat hoac qua gian deu kho doc.
line-lengthGioi han 65-75 ky tu moi dong. Qua dai se moi mat. Dung max-width: 65ch.
font-pairingHeading va Body nen khac "tinh cach": Heading dam, manh me + Body nhe, de doc.
animation-durationMicro-interaction: 150-300ms. Qua nhanh nguoi dung khong thay, qua cham gay kho chiu.
transform-onlyChi animate transform va opacity. Tranh animate width, height, margin vi gay giat.

Nen lamKhong nen lam

58 Phong Cach Thiet Ke UI

Quy Trinh 4 Buoc

Day la quy trinh ma Antigravity thuc hien khi ban yeu cau thiet ke giao dien:

Buoc 1: Phan tich yeu cau

Xac dinh loai san pham, keyword phong cach, nganh nghe, va tech stack dang dung.

Buoc 2: Tao Design System (bat buoc)

Chay lenh --design-system de search dong thoi 5 linh vuc: product, style, color, landing, typography. Ket qua la mot bo thiet ke day du.

Buoc 3: Bo sung tim kiem chi tiet

Neu can tinh chinh, tim kiem them theo tung domain cu the (style, color, typography, ux).

Buoc 4: Ap dung quy tac theo Stack

Tu dong ap dung cac quy tac rieng cua stack dang su dung (React, Next.js, Flutter...).

Vi Du Cu The

Vi du 1: Tao landing page cho spa

# Ban chi can noi:
"Tao trang landing page cho tiem spa cao cap, phong cach sang trong"

# Antigravity se tu dong:
1. Chon style: Glassmorphism hoac Minimalism
2. Chon mau: Vang gold + trang kem + xanh la nhat
3. Chon font: Playfair Display (heading) + Inter (body)
4. Ap dung: touch target 44px, contrast 4.5:1, lazy loading...

Vi du 2: Thiet ke dashboard SaaS

# Ban noi:
"Thiet ke trang dashboard cho phan mem quan ly du an"

# Antigravity se:
1. Chon style: Dark Mode hoac Minimalism
2. Chon mau: Xanh duong #2563EB + cam #F97316 cho CTA
3. Chon font: Plus Jakarta Sans (chuyen dung cho SaaS)
4. Ap dung: z-index scale, skeleton loading, responsive grid...

Checklist Truoc Khi Ship

Danh sach kiem tra cuoi cung truoc khi giao san pham cho khach hang hoac deploy len production:

Chat luong hinh anh

  • Khong dung emoji lam icon (phai dung SVG)
  • Tat ca icon cung 1 bo (Heroicons hoac Lucide)
  • Logo thuong hieu chinh xac
  • Hover khong lam xo layout

Tuong tac

  • Moi phan tu click duoc co cursor-pointer
  • Hover co phan hoi truc quan ro rang
  • Transition muot (150-300ms)
  • Focus state hien thi cho keyboard nav

Light/Dark Mode

  • Chu co do tuong phan du (4.5:1 toi thieu)
  • Card/glass hien thi tot tren ca 2 mode
  • Vien (border) nhin thay duoc tren ca 2 mode
  • Da test thu ca 2 mode truoc khi giao

Layout & Responsive

  • Khong bi noi dung bi che boi navbar co dinh
  • Responsive tot tai 375px, 768px, 1024px, 1440px
  • Khong cuon ngang tren mobile
  • Touch target toi thieu 44x44px

Accessibility

  • Moi hinh anh co alt text
  • O nhap lieu co label
  • Mau sac khong phai dau hieu duy nhat
  • Ton trong prefers-reduced-motion
